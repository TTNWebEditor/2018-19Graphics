Most Temple students apply successfuly for FAFSA

Temple has more than 26,600 undergraduate students, and nearly 90 percent of them filled out the FAFSA in the 2017-18 academic year.








20,700 undergraduate students, or 71 percent of the undergraduate student body, filed the 2017-18 FAFSA and received financial aid.
3,118 undergraduate students, or 11 percent of the undergraduate student body, filed the 2017-18 FAFSA but did not receive financial aid.
5,317 undergraduate students, or 18 percent of the undergraduate student body, did not file the 2017-18 FAFSA.
